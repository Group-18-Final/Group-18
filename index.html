About

Success for the disabled is very important, as many who are disabled feel they will be judged or made fun of just because of their disability. This will cause them to not want to work, which leads to them not having a job. Is this really how we want people with disabilities to feel? Now, I don't personally have a disability, but I would not feel good if this is how I thought others looked at me. There is so much talent that is wasted every year by companies and corporations on disabled people just because they are disabled and they don't believe that they can do things at the level that people without disabilities can. As a society, we should be more welcoming and inclusive to the disabled to encourage them to want to go out and get a job because, like stated above, there are so many disabled people that have talent and a good work ethic, but companies and corporations don't see that in them because they are disabled.

The Disability Problem May Be an Unemployment Problem in Disguise

Misericordia provides housing, jobs, and volunteer opportunities for people with disabilities. The goal of misericordia is to provide people with disabilities a fair and honest opportunity in the workplace and life as a whole. Misericordia also aids people with disabilities in finding in store jobs, and outsourced work that can be completed from their residence. They are a non-profit organization that obtains all their funding from donations and volunteers. Providing people with disabilities a fair opportunity to enjoy work without stereotypes truly is a step forward to ending discrimination towards people with disabilities in the workplace

Working From Different Places

Many companies across a variety of industries offer positions that give employees the option to work from home. Positions in marketing and computer operations are commonly able to be filled from outside an office, and this structure of work has grown even more available since the Covid-19 pandemic. Some companies have changed their work atmosphere entirely by eliminating the in-office option. Airbnb, Spotify, and Dropbox all have openings in various positions in various departments that are permanently filled from home. Working from home could be a great opportunity for someone with disabilities that prevent them from comfortably working in an office. We hope that in the future more companies make accommodations for positions that have the potential to be filled from home in order to help equalize opportunity as much as possible. .

Dealing with discrimination

A big part of why disabled people struggle to find jobs are because of discriminatory employers. These employers may not want to expend resources on making sure disabled people are able to work in their environment. The EEOC (Equal Employment Opportunity Commission) exists to help with discrimination. While a lot of discrimination happens by the rejection of disabled workers, there are still resources that will help in the instance that people are not receiving the accommodations needed or are being discriminated against in another way. This can help to prevent additional discrimination and help those who need accommodations.
